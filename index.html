  • Episode 1 - "Welcome to the Playground"

    Vi and Powder, orphaned sisters from Zaun, struggle for survival while committing petty crimes. A high-stakes heist in Piltover goes wrong, triggering a citywide manhunt. Tensions between the rich upper city and the oppressed undercity intensify as dangerous forces start to surface, threatening the sisters' fragile world.

  • Episode 2 - "Some Mysteries Are Better Left Unsolved"

    Jayce faces expulsion from Piltover's Academy after a dangerous experiment with arcane magic fails. Viktor, an outcast assistant, takes interest in his work. Meanwhile, Vi and her friends evade the enforcers, while Silco, a criminal mastermind, plots to overthrow Piltover, seeing opportunity in the escalating chaos.

  • Episode 3 - "The Base Violence Necessary for Change"

    A deadly confrontation between Vi’s crew and Silco’s forces ends in tragedy when Powder’s attempt to help causes devastating destruction. Vi is captured, and Powder, feeling abandoned, is taken in by Silco. Meanwhile, Jayce earns unexpected support, securing his future in Piltover’s political and scientific world.
